Introducing INTERXDB — Barbados' First Exclusive Retail Auction & Deals App

Dear Business Partner,

On behalf of INTERXDB, we are pleased to extend this exclusive invitation to your organisation to become one of the founding retail partners on Barbados' first dedicated business-to-consumer auction and deals platform.

INTERXDB owns and operates the complete digital infrastructure — the platform, the mobile application, all hosting, and all technical services. You simply list, we handle the rest.

The platform is designed with one purpose: to keep your store as the buzz word in every household, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — whether your doors are open or not. Customers compete for your products through live auctions, driving excitement, urgency, and loyalty to your brand.

How the Platform Works

01
App-Only Marketplace
All auctions, Deals of the Week, and Deals of the Month are conducted exclusively through the INTERXDB mobile app. No auctions appear on the public website.
02
Registered Businesses Only
Only verified, registered retail businesses may list products. No individual or private sellers are permitted. This protects your brand and ensures a professional marketplace.
03
25-Item Weekly Limit
Each participating store is allocated up to 25 items per week on the app. This controlled supply model creates scarcity, drives urgency, and maximises bidding competition.
04
Free Customer Registration
End-users (customers) register on the app at no cost. Stores are required to promote the app in-store and across their social media and marketing channels to their existing customer base.

Partner Obligations & Platform Conduct

Participating stores agree to the following conditions upon registration:

Store Partner Responsibilities

  • Actively promote the INTERXDB app in-store (signage, POS materials) and across all social media and marketing channels directed at their customer base.
  • Ensure all listed products are accurately described, available, and fulfilled upon auction completion.
  • Honour winning auction bids — failure to fulfil confirmed orders will result in suspension or removal from the platform.
  • Maintain professional standards of communication with winning customers via the platform.

User Conduct: Registered customers who fail to honour their obligations — including confirmed auction wins — will be banned from the platform. Disputes are subject to review, and reasonable evidence from both parties will be considered before any action is taken.
