Repossessed Vehicles

Cars, SUVs, trucks, motorcycles, and commercial vehicles recovered from defaulted loans. Each unit listed separately with photos, year, make, model, mileage, and reserve price. Bidding opens immediately.

Foreclosed Properties

Residential and commercial properties in foreclosure. Listed as Deal of the Month for maximum 30-day exposure. Full legal descriptions, photos, and title details provided by your team and displayed to all registered BiziBid users.
